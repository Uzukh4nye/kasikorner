Kasi Korner is born from the everyday experience of growing up in a South African township, and knowing there has to be a better way.

our story.

Growing up in the townships, going to the spaza shop was part of daily life. But it always came with uncertainty: you never knew if the item you needed was in stock, or if the price had changed since last time.

Being sent by your mother to buy spices, arriving at the spaza and finding out they don't have it, walking to another one, arriving home with the wrong thing... that experience of uncertainty is something every township resident knows.

Kasi Korner was built to solve that. Not through delivery, not through replacing the spaza, but by giving the community the information they need before they leave home.

Township commerce has always been the backbone of community life. We're just helping it be seen.
